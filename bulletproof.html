Bullet Proof Glass


In fact , bullet proof glass is one kind of the laminated glasses . It is formed by multiple layers of glasses (Organic or inorganic)and films . It is a composition which can stop the bullet goes through in the weapon range and protect for human body and anti-theft , and also remains the features of a normal laminated glass .

Bullet Proof principle
When a high-speed bullet impacts on the bulletproof glass , the hitting position of the glass breaks and forms a radial shape , but the bullet head will be deformed . The kinetic energy of the bullet will be reduced and absorbed .The kinetic energy of the bullet will be reduced and absorbed make it impossible to penetrate the glass , while the glass pieces are stuck , making it difficult to spatter and injure others .
